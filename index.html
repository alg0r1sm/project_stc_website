Save the Children exists to help every child reach their full potential. In Wales, the U.K. and around the world, we make sure children stay safe, healthy and keep learning so they can become who they want to be.

We are a local group of volunteers on Anglesey and in the Bangor area who aim to raise as much as possible to support the work of Save the Children here in Wales, the U.K and worldwide. We welcome anyone who would like to volunteer with us. Whatever your skills or interests there’s a place for you.

WAYS TO JOIN IN

PLANNING MEETINGS

We meet monthly in the cafe in M’Sparc in Gaerwen to plan different events aimed at ‘something for everyone’. The meetings are open to anyone and we welcome new ideas.

OUR SUPPORTERS

Not keen on meetings? We have a team of fantastic supporters who we really rely on to help us with fundraising events. We tell them what we are up to and they help out when they can.Something as simple as baking a cake or rattling a tin makes a real contribution.

SPONSORED EVENTS

From time to time we organise or take part in sponsored events. We’ve walked, run and even zip wired to support Save’s work. Might you be interested? Are you a member of a team, club or group of friends that might be interested in fundraising for us in a sponsored event?

INDIVIDUAL INITIATIVES

Another option is to set up a fundraiser of your own which we can support you with if you need us to or guide you to someone in the national office who can help.

WAYS WE HAVE RECENTLY FUNDRAISED

  • an annual, highly successful craft fair in Trearddur Bay.
  • most years, an auction of goods and promises with dancing to live music in partnership with local businesses and well known Welsh band, Y Moniars.
  • collections at local football and rugby clubs.
  • cake and jewellery sales at Holyhead Sailing Club.
  • a Christmas collection at Waitrose supported by Menai Bridge Band.
  • a carol concert with children from Ysgol y Borth and Ysgol Llanfairpwll.
  • sponsored events.
  • And much, much more!

A gig in the Iorwerth Arms in Bryngwran organised on our behalf by local band, Brodyr Magee, raised lots of funds for our work in Ukraine. Our latest coffee morning in Menai Bridge raised almost £1800 for our appeal for Turkey and Syria!

AS WELL AS FUNDRAISING

  • Some of us give talks about Save the Children’s work to local groups,chapels, churches and schools.
  • From time to time we run special meetings with national speakers on issues in the news such as the refugee camps in Calais, the situation in Yemen etc.
  • We liaise with our local M.P.s and Senedd representatives.

NATIONAL EVENTS

We get involved with all - Wales events too. These have included a sponsored walk in which Welsh volunteers covered the whole Wales Coastal Path…3 times! A team of Welsh volunteers raised funds by daring the famous Zipwire. We raised over £5000 selling preloved jewellery donated by women throughout Wales at the Eisteddfod in 2022.

And of course we put on silly jumpers for Save the Children U.K’s annual CHRISTMAS JUMPER DAY!!
